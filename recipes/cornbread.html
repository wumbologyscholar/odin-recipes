Cornbread

Description

This is a family-style recipe for a delicious batch of cornbread that takes 45 minutes total to make, and serves 12. With the holiday season quickly approaching, this dish is sure to be the hit of the potluck, and leave your guests barely even questioning the fact that you "forgot" to get anyone gifts this year ;).

Ingredients

  • 3 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 0.5 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 3 eggs
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 cup of butter, melted
  • 2 cups frozen corn kernels, thawed (optional)
