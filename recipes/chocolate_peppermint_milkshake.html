Chocolate Peppermint Milkshake

Description

An amazing recipe of a quick and tasty winter treat. It only takes around 10 minutes to make, and depending on the amaounts of ingredients used, can serve 1 or many.

Ingredients

  • 5 ice cubes
  • 5 hard peppermint candies
  • 1 tablespoon dark chocolate chips (such as Ghirardelli 60% Dark Chocolate)
  • 4 scoops softened vanilla bean ice cream, or more to taste
  • 0.5 teaspoon peppermint extract, or to taste
  • 3 drops red food coloring
  • 0.5 cup skim milk, or as needed

Steps

  1. Blend ice cubes, peppermint candies, and chocolate chips together in a blender until evenly blended and slightly grainy.
  2. Add ice cream, peppermint extract, and red food coloring; blend until smooth.
  3. Add milk and blend until desired consistency is reached.
  4. Enjoy!
